Mission Statement

This website will increase the number of members, enthusiastic volunteers to help out during music concerts, number of concert tickets sold, and the number of musicians performing at concerts.

Success Evaluation

The success of the website will be evaluated by the amount of new members that have signed up, and also by the amount of people (members and non members) that volunteer to help with future concerts. The success will also be evaluted by the amount of tickets that are being sold to future conncerts. And overall more involvement from musicians.(Concert musicians and House Musicians)

Target Audience

The target audience is adults and young adults who are music enthusiasts, like music of all genres, as well as anyone significantly involved in music in any way.

    • Content on Home page-

    A summarised list of upcoming events ordering from the closest event date to events that are planned for the near future. This list of events will only display a maximum of 4 at a time and give a very brief summary of the event as well as the date of the event,location of the event and a "BUY TICKETS" call to action if tickets are available for that event. In the page's footer on the left hand side you will find the website's Contact details.

    • Content on Events page-

    A full and detailed list of upcoming events ordering from the closest event date to events that are planned for the future. This list of events will display about the same amount of information on each event as the Home page, however once you click on the event, it will take you to a different page with all details on the specific event such as artists that whill be playing, a schedule for the event etc. In the page's footer on the left hand side you will find the website's Contact details.

    • Content on Artists page-

    A list of all of the artists that are currently registered as active musicians at the Townsville Community Music Centre. The list will show a thumbnail picture of each artist as well as their name and the title of the next event they will be performing in. Once you click on their names it will take you to a new page which will show more indepth and detailed information such as past events they've performed in, any musical instruments they are skilled at, future events they're planned to perform at etc. In the page's footer on the left hand side you will find the website's Contact details.
